Health Canada recently issued an important update concerning the Hotline IV Fluid Warmer System and its accessories. These critical medical devices play a vital role in patient care, particularly during surgeries, trauma, and in intensive care units, by safely warming intravenous fluids and blood products before administration. This helps prevent hypothermia, a common complication that can lead to adverse outcomes like increased bleeding, slower recovery, and higher infection risk.
